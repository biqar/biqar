Hi, my name is Abdullah Al Raqibul Islam. I usually go as Raqib in real life and biqar/biqarboy in cyberspace. I am a Researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Ohio State University and a member of the [IDT Lab](https://idtlab.github.io) (PI: [Prof. Suren Byna](https://sbyna.github.io)). My research focuses on designing scalable, high-performance systems for data-intensive applications, with an emphasis on reducing data movement bottlenecks in modern architectures.

I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from UNC Charlotte in December 2025. [My dissertation](https://www.proquest.com/docview/3281144257?pq-origsite=gscholar&fromopenview=true&sourcetype=Dissertations%20&%20Theses) developed high-performance data structures for efficient sparse storage and computation. Prior to academia, I worked as a Principal Software Engineer at [TigerIT Bangladesh Ltd.](https://www.tigerit.com), leading the development of machine learning-driven and large-scale data management systems. I earned my bachelor’s degree in Computer Science from [University of Dhaka, Bangladesh (DU)](https://www.cse.du.ac.bd).

### Research Interest
- AI-Driven Optimization for High-Performance Systems
- Parallel and High-Performance Computing
- Scalable Sparse Data Management
- Intelligent Data Management for Modern Hardware Architectures
